The 12 Main Roman Gods

The Roman pantheon features twelve principal gods who were adapted from Greek mythology with different names.

  • Jupiter - King of the Gods
  • Juno - Goddess of Marriage
  • Neptune - God of the Sea
  • Ceres - Goddess of the Harvest
  • Minerva - Goddess of Wisdom
  • Apollo - God of the Sun and Music
  • Diana - Goddess of the Hunt
  • Mars - God of War
  • Venus - Goddess of Love
  • Vulcan - God of Fire and Forge
  • Mercury - Messenger of the Gods
  • Vesta - Goddess of Hearth and Home
